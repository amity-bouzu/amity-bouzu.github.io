Video Project 4


I don't actually remember what the requirements for this assignment were - and unfortunately, the syllabus from the semester I took this class doesn't tell me. But I do remember what I was thinking when I first made it. I wanted to make a video that spoke to the idea of people holding each other, of helping one another - hence the title, "Up."

I chose to represent this idea through the physical challenge that is walking up 10 flights of stairs - I was living in Joe West at the time, which is where I recorded - and as our unknown protagonist kept climbing up the stairs, they would meet others who'd climb up with them. As they met these others, they would support one another - shown through how the people hug, how they begin to move faster, and how color begins to come into what is originally a black and white video.

I'm very pleased with how this video came out.
