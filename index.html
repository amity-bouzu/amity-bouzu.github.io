Artist Statement


I am a Japanese American artist who explores different digital mediums to make different types of art, that I hope to use to influence society for the better. Most notably I am a storyteller, illustrator, animator, and 3D modeler. My ultimate goal in life is to make comics in Japan (become a mangaka).

My art is often influenced, or meant to work with the stories I write. My stories are often themed or centered around my experiences of being queer, neurodivergent and of mixed race. Due to my desire to work in Japan, these themes play a central role in my work - I want to make Japan a more open space for those that are considered different, or other - to disrupt the homogeny, to disrupt the society that forces people into neat boxes without ever letting anyone be individual.

Through my art, I express my stories. And soon, people will listen.
