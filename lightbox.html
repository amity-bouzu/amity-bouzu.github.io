Shadow Box

This is a light box that I made with a friend, based on a story that we are writing together. The character in the center is a member of the country's council, so we thought it would be interesting to show said character in the midst of a council meeting. Above the silhouettes of the people is supposed to be a giant window that represents each of the regions of this nation.
