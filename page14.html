Pale as a sheet– Eyes rapidly lose their glimmer– Hands sturdy but only twitching–

Please.

Please. This can’t be– not you. Not you too!
Thin fingers stained, a steady weight from above – stem the flow
Your blue dress gone purple, clutched close, hair tickling
Tears escaping but smiling, the corners of your mouth tugged up just a hair

And you know, but you shouldn’t know, you shouldn’t be saying those words here–
