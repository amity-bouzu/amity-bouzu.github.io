Video Project 3


For this project, we were meant to make something that was more abstract, that also involved the use of sound. I decided that origami would be a good choice - I could fold the origami in colors that were the queer flags, to match my theme, (for this video I chose the pride rainbow, bisexual, and transgender flags), and I could also include sound through the folding noises. I ended up with something more ASMR than initially intended, and my video did run a little long - I would probably change it some if I were to redo it - but I am happy with how it turned out.
