Audio Project

The third project in Art 75 was the audio assignment. To make an audio about being queer, I thought the simplest, most effective choice would be to make a recording about what the experience of realizing that you're LGBTQIA+ is like.

As I thought about it more, though, I thought it would be interesting if my audio included the stories of more than one person - and instead was a crowd, or a group of people - because the experience of being queer, of finding out you're queer - that's an experience that many many people have. And I wanted my audio to reflect that.

I decided to make the first forty seconds just one person - because I did actually want at least part of a queer story to be told, or listened to, before it would become unintelligible through the words of all the others as they were added. After those forty seconds passed, I added new voices, one by one - and if you listen closely, you can, in fact, pick out individual stories. But that's not the point. The point is that all of these people, together, are speaking about their queer experience.
