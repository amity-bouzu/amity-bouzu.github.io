Walk, Run, Jump

Basing characters off of mythical creatures is something I enjoy doing. This particular character is a dragon-girl named Amelo. I made her 3D model using a combination of Maya, Zbrush, and Substance painter, then rigged and animated her to walk, run, and jump in Maya.
