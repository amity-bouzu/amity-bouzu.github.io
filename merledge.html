Merman Legend

I wanted to draw this particular character (Legend, from the Legend of Zelda fancomic Linked Universe) as a merman. For this piece, I began to render and shade in a way that I never have before - experimenting with light sources wasn't something I'd ever really done, so I was very excited when this started coming together, as it looked way better than I was ever expecting it to.
