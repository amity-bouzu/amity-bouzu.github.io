Net Art

For this project, I thought that it would be fun to make my net art be literally poetic - as in, I chose to have my net art be a poem. I'd already written a poem for a different class, so I thought it would be interesting to make the poem a more interactive experience- where you have to click on either the images or the text itself in order to hear more. I'm not sure if I made the greatest website, but I think I was able to make an interesting interactive-poem, and I'm pretty happy with how it turned out.
